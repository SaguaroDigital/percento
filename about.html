About Percento

Percento is a free, fast, and simple percentage calculator designed for everyday use. Whether you're checking a discount at a store, verifying a figure at work, or helping with homework — Percento gives you the answer instantly, without clutter or complexity.

Why Percento?

Most percentage calculators on the web are buried inside bloated apps or cluttered with distractions. Percento was built around natural way people think about percentage logic.
